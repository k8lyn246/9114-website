Meet Our Divisions

01

Design

The design group is responsible for designing the robot and making sure it works. We create detailed CAD models with integrated mechanical and electrical systems and make modifications based on testing and review feedback.

02

Mechanical

The creation of the actual body and parts that move—frame, arms, wheels. We look after all assembly and maintenance so everything mechanical works together.

03

Electrical

We manage wiring, sensors, and overall power systems. We install and adjust electrical elements, handle power distribution, and repair failures as needed.

04

Software

We design and code autonomous routines that run without human control for the first 15 seconds of a match, and build the teleop control interface for drivers during the rest of the game—combining sensor data, path planning, and real-time response.

05

Outreach

We partner with local businesses and sponsors for funding and resources, and collaborate with the community to promote STEM education through outreach events, presentations, and demonstrations—all in the spirit of FIRST Robotics.
