Who Are We?

FRC 9114, the Rotationaries, is a community-based, student-led robotics team in Fremont, CA. We were founded in the summer of 2022 and have competed in the FIRST Robotics Challenge for 4 seasons. Our team hopes to bridge technical expertise, community involvement, and an inclusive culture for all members, giving real-world experience in a hands-on environment.
